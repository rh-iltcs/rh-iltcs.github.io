About WOCMAT

There are seven IRCAM experts (including IRCAM Director, Frank Madlener) will participate 「2016 Joint WOCMAT - IRCAM Forum Conference」, and it is the 2nd time IRCAM Forum@Asia! All of the Music Technology with New Trends will be presented in Taiwan!

「2016 國際電腦音樂與音訊技術暨法國IRCAM工作坊聯合研討會​​」邀請法國龐畢度音樂與聲學研究中心IRCAM包括主任等八位頂尖研究人員來台灣親自展示最新的音樂科技發展技術，這是IRCAM中心第二次在亞洲舉辦的論壇，絕對物超所值，不容錯過！
